* = Caretaker Manager.

a^ = The official Liverpool website lists Barclay and McKenna as joint managers.

Barclay held the post of "secretary-manager" and McKenna held the post of "coach-manager".

b^a.b. = Kenny Dalglish served two terms as manager. His cumulative managerial statistics read as:-

Record (P381 W222 D95 L64 F847 A406 W%58.27) Honours (L13 L20 FA2 LC1 CS4 EC0 UC0 US0 WC0 Total10).

c^ = Souness underwent heart surgery in April 1992, and Ronnie Moran took charge of the team until the 1992 FA Cup Final.

d^ = Houllier was absent from October 2001 to February 2002, due to illness.

During this time, Phil Thompson stepped in as temporary manager (P33 W16 D12 L5). These matches are included in Houllier's record. }

ABBREVIATIONS:-

Name = Managers name.

Nat = Nation manager is from.

From = Beginning of management for Liverpool.

To = End of management for Liverpool.

Pld = How many games managed.

W = How many games won.

D = How many games drawn.

L = How many games lost.

GF = How many goals scored.

GA = How many goals against.

Win% = Percentage of games won to drawn and lost.

L1 = Premiership and old League One.

L2 = Championship and old League Two.

FA = F.A. Cup.

LC = League Cup.

CS = Charity Shield.

EU = Old European Cup or Champions League

UC = Uefa Cup.

US = Uefa Super Cup

WC = World Football Cup

Ttl = Total of trophies won
